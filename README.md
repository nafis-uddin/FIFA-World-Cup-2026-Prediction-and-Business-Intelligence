# FIFA World Cup 2026 Prediction & Business Intelligence

A collaborative Machine Learning and Business Intelligence project that predicts FIFA World Cup 2026 outcomes using historical football data while generating strategic business insights for sponsorship, marketing, and fan engagement.

## Project Objectives

- Predict FIFA World Cup 2026 match outcomes
- Forecast tournament progression
- Analyze historical international football performance
- Build predictive Machine Learning models
- Generate Business Intelligence insights for commercial decision-making

---

## Dataset

Historical international football match data including:

- FIFA Rankings
- Match Results
- Goals Scored
- Team Statistics
- Tournament History
- Team Form
- Head-to-Head Records
